About Cats Corner

Our Story

Cats Corner was founded with a simple yet profound love for felines. We believe that every cat deserves to look and feel their best, and every cat lover deserves a special place to connect with these wonderful creatures. Our journey began with a passion for creating a unique space where professional grooming services meet the delightful atmosphere of a cat cafe.

We are a haven for both cats and their humans, offering top-notch grooming to keep your furbabies healthy and happy, and a cozy environment where you can relax, enjoy a treat, and spend time with our charming resident cats.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional and compassionate grooming services for cats, ensuring their comfort and well-being. Simultaneously, to offer a welcoming and relaxing cat cafe experience where guests can enjoy delicious refreshments and the therapeutic presence of our beloved cats, fostering a community of cat enthusiasts.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every cat with the utmost care and kindness.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in both grooming and cafe services.
  • Community: We aim to create a friendly and engaging space for all cat lovers.
  • Cleanliness: A pristine environment for both our feline residents and human guests.
