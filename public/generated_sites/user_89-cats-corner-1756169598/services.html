Our Professional Grooming Services

Pamper Your Feline Friend

At Cats Corner, we understand that grooming is essential for your cat's health and happiness. Our experienced and gentle groomers provide a stress-free environment, ensuring your beloved cat receives the best care possible. We use high-quality, cat-safe products and techniques tailored to your cat's individual needs.

Luxury Bath & Brush

A soothing bath with premium shampoos and conditioners, followed by a thorough brush-out to remove loose fur and prevent matting. Leaves your cat's coat shiny and soft.

Nail Trimming

Keep your cat's paws healthy and your furniture safe with our professional nail trimming service. Performed gently and efficiently.

De-Shedding Treatment

Reduce shedding and hairballs with our specialized de-shedding treatment, leaving your cat's coat lighter and healthier.
