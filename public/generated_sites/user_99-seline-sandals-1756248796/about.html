Our Story: Where Tradition Meets Trend

Seline Sandals was born from a deep appreciation for the vibrant Maasai culture and the incredible artistry of its women. We are a Kenyan-owned brand dedicated to bringing authentic, handcrafted Maasai sandals to young women who value unique style, cultural heritage, and ethical fashion.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to empower Maasai women artisans by providing a sustainable platform for their exquisite beadwork and leather craftsmanship. Each pair of Seline Sandals is a testament to generations of skill, passed down through families, imbued with stories and traditions.

We believe in fashion that makes a difference. By choosing Seline Sandals, you're not just buying a beautiful pair of shoes; you're supporting fair trade practices, celebrating indigenous artistry, and contributing to the economic empowerment of women in rural Kenyan communities. Walk with purpose, walk with Seline Sandals.
